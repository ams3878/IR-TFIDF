Search Engine of the My Little Pony episodes/movie scripts.

Useful for finding words/phrases in documents.

Advanced Search capabilities are available using the '#' preceding a query.

Available commands:

#episdoes - returns a list of every episode title in order of episodes number

#characters - returns a list of every "speaker" (the bold names left of the line) from the scripts.

#episode(n) - where (n) is replaced with an episode number, and will navigate straight to that script's html
